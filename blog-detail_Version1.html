5 Running Tips for Beginners

2025-10-01 · by Nike Team

Starting your running journey? Here are 5 essential tips to get you moving safely and effectively:

  • Set Realistic Goals: Start with short, achievable distances and gradually increase.
  • Warm Up & Cool Down: Always stretch and do light exercises before and after running.
  • Choose the Right Shoes: Wearing the correct footwear prevents injuries and improves comfort.
  • Stay Consistent: Regular practice is the key to improvement.
  • Listen to Your Body: Rest when you feel pain or discomfort.

Remember, every athlete was once a beginner. Just do it!
