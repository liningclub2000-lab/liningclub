Our Story

1964

Founded

Nike was founded by Bill Bowerman and Phil Knight as Blue Ribbon Sports.

1971

Nike Brand

The company officially became Nike, named after the Greek goddess of victory.

1985

Air Jordan

Partnership with Michael Jordan launched the iconic Air Jordan line.

1988

Just Do It

The legendary "Just Do It" campaign was launched, becoming one of the most recognized slogans in history.

Global Leader
